# ShopScout Terms

Last updated: 2026-07-20

ShopScout is provided as a product research and comparison aid. It helps users capture product facts, organize lists, normalize product attributes, and prepare AI-assisted buying analysis.

## User Responsibility

Users are responsible for verifying purchase decisions, prices, warranties, return policies, seller reliability, and product suitability before buying.

## AI Output

AI analysis may be incomplete or incorrect. ShopScout labels AI workflows as decision support, not as authoritative advice. Users should verify important claims against manufacturer, retailer, or authoritative sources.

## Website Access

ShopScout captures product information only when the user initiates capture or scan actions. Users are responsible for complying with applicable website terms and local laws.

## No Purchase Relationship

ShopScout does not sell products, process purchases, handle payments, or provide warranties.

## Changes

The extension may be updated to improve capture, normalization, comparison, and AI workflows.
